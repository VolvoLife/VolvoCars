In 1956 the car was presented by Volvos research and development team. It is known as the Amazon in Nordic countrys like Norway & Sweden but in the rest of the world it is known as the 121 and the 122S. It was much larger than its previous model the PV444. The first Amazon was shown at the Stockholm Motor Show in 1962 it has 118 horse power and Volvo produced 667,332 Amazons.
