Volvo is a Swedish car manufacter in Gothenburg but recently was acuired by a company called Geeley. Geeley holds majority shares and the rest is owned by Ford Motor company. They manufacter Station Wagons, SUVs, Sedans as well as semi-trucks. They are pioneers is motor transportation saftey and federal goverments took notice. They invented the 3 point seat belt and are know to be the first car manufacter to standardize this on all their cars in a time where vechicle safety was not a priority. They are also known as being one of the earliest manufactuers with airbags standardized. They have many types of cars for different consumers needs.
